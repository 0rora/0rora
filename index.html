Scheduling

Settle pending payments at exactly the right time by specifying future payment dates. Payments will remain warehoused until they're due.

Authorized users can manage payment schedules, by editing, deleting or rescheduling payments.

High Throughput

Payment channel coordination is achieved by using multiple user accounts to ensure that high throughput is achieved and maintained.

Payments that are due around the same time are batched to improve performance and reduce transaction fees.

Resilience

On-network errors are detected and repaired automatically where possible. Transactions that fail are retried after error detection and correction.

Admin users are alerted to potential errors, such as an account having insufficient funds to cover future payments.

Account management
